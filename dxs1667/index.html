Hey there! My name is David. I grew up in a modest suburb of Chicago called Bartlett. After graduating from South Elgin High School, I attended the University of Wisconsin-Madison where I majored in astrophysics, mathematics and physics, earning my diploma in May 2021. The defining aspect of my undergraduate experience was the research assistantship I undertook with Professor Robert Mathieu in the Department of Astronomy.
In May 2023 I graduated from the University of Southern California, ...earning a Master of Science in computer science. (My cat Henry really should receive an honorary degree because of all his support!) I am an amateur plant hobbyist, avid hiker and enthusiastic gamer. I also enjoy cooking meals from various cuisines, though Asian and Mexican are my favorite.
